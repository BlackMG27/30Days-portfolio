    About Me

    I am a results-driven designer with over two years of experience in web design, front-end development, storyboarding, wireframing, and out-of-the-box problem solving to offer multi-tasking capabilities and efficiently produce design projects.

    I am a creator who enjoys giving others great experiences and memories through my creations. I want to know why something is the way it is, or the root of the problem, and how to make it better.

    When I get a design or development project, I relish the challenge of creating something new while putting some of my own personality into the mix. Furthermore, understanding the user is king when dealing with clients and I always like to ask questions and sharing a story so that I can better understand their problem.

    I enjoy self-reflection and meditation especially when I am traveling. Traveling lets me get out of my own little world and care more about the one that is inhabited by 7,000,000,000+ people.

    Moreover, I've been told that I'm one hell of a baker!
